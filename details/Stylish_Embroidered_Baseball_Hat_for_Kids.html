Farm-Thematic Baseball Hat for Kids

This embroidered baseball hat is crafted to combine comfort and style for young boys and girls who adore farm life. The charming green tractor embroidery adds a fun, personalized touch that kids will love wearing. Designed with soft and lightweight washed cotton, it feels gentle on the skin and remains cool throughout the activities kids enjoy—all day, every day.

The adjustable strapback featuring an antique brass buckle ensures a secure and customizable fit for children of different head sizes. Breathable panels and moisture-absorbent sweatband greatly enhance the comfort, making it ideal for outdoor play, family outings, or sports events—all weather conditions included.
