Gymboree Girls' and Toddler Pull on Leggings

These Gymboree pull-on leggings are designed with a soft, breathable fabric that keeps your little one comfortable all day long. The subtle lilac flower print adds a touch of playful charm, making them a favorite for both casual wear and active play. Pair effortlessly with any Gymboree top for a coordinated look that's as stylish as it is functional.

Soft Fabric

Our leggings are crafted from a blend of cotton and elastane, ensuring a smooth, flexible feel that moves with your child. This soft fabric is gentle on sensitive skin and perfect for all-day wear without irritation.

Cotton Fabric

With a focus on comfort and breathability, the cotton fabric offers a cozy and adaptive fit. It's ideal for little adventurers who need freedom of movement while staying stylish.

Printed Design

The cheerful lilac flower print brings a sense of whimsical fun, perfect for adding a splash of color and personality to your child's wardrobe. It’s a subtle yet eye-catching detail that makes every outfit feel special.

Comfortable Fit

Designed with an elasticized waistband, these leggings provide a secure and comfortable fit. They make getting dressed quick and easy, ideal for busy parents and playful toddlers.

Flowery Fun

The flowing design of these leggings complements a wide range of Gymboree tops, creating a cohesive and fun look for any occasion. Whether it's a day at the park or a cozy playdate, these leggings are a great choice.

Sustainable Wearables

These stylish, sustainable toddler leggings are perfect for long-lasting playtime. Their soft, breathable materials ensure your child stays comfortable, while the playful print adds a unique touch to every outfit.

Create a fun and functional wardrobe for your toddler with Gymboree pull-on leggings that blend comfort, style, and sustainability. The perfect match for any day, these leggings are a must-have for playful little ones.
